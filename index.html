About Our App

A one stop solution for creating your own customed design to improve your phorotgraphy skills. An application that aims to promote the best outcome quality of your creative work. Explore the new features to help you intelligently organize and edit your photos. Share your photographs with loved ones, clients, or an even bigger audience.

Fully Responsive

Gives the user the ease to create amazing photos, anywhere you are. Dazzle is the cloud-based service that gives you everything you need to create, edit, organize, store, and share your photos across any device.

Flexible

Easy-to-use tools like sliders and presets let you create photos that look just the way you want. Start on mobile, web, or desktop- your edits will be automatically applied everywhere else. Provides great storage which ensures that you can access your library wherever you are.

Stylish Design

Dazzle can manage any design built by its users, it can also organize any number of photos so you'll always stay great and on top of your game. Capture, edit, and share your photos all from one place.

Discovery

Dazzle provides a page where the real magic happens. Curated collections, organized by content such as portraiture, nature scenes, or by season, are used to showcase user creation and drive interaction on the platform.

Designed to Inspire

Connect with fellow photoraphers and deepen your skills in the preview of the new Dazzle community. Explore inspirational content, share your favorite edits, and get presets from your peers.

In-app Tutorials

Interactive tutorials available on both desktop and mobile help you master new skills without ever leaving Dazzle. Provide easy steps on which you can improve the way you create an amazing photo in order for you to become an expert in your field your wanting to- photography.

How The App Works?

Sign-Up

Once Dazzle is installed in user's device, they need to sign up for an account in order for users to have an access to its unlimited features. Dazzle provides a 1 week free tutorial for its user to have an experience with the whole features it has and after that, users can avail premium access for a hassle free usage of the applicaction.

Upload

Uploading photos and videos acts as your repository for both unedited and edited content imported to the app or taken with the Dazzle camera. The upload section is divorced from a user's profile which means it is completely private, for your eyes only, until a post is published.

Create

Dazzle's wide-range of unique filters are arguably the app's biggest draw. With over 200 presets available, users will undoubtedly find the best filter and color for theirpicture of choice. Aside from filters, Dazzle lets users incorporate all sorts of effects. Add borders, text, or vignette. Users can also manually adjust your image's dimensions, exposure, contrast, sharpness, and clarity as well.

Publish

This is where users get to post their creation in public, as well as repost photos from other users in their feed. Use it as a mood board, vision board, to document every moment that comes to you. Once your work is published, it has the chance to reach tons of audience which will bring you on the top of your dreams.
